Blue Bear School of music. Non-profit Organization.

Blue Bear School of Music was founded as “Blue Bear Waltzes School of Genuine Music” in the summer of 1971 by a rock band called “Wolfgang & Strauss.”

As a nonprofit organization, Blue Bear partners with schools, shelters, and community centers to bring the transformative power of playing music to underserved populations for FREE.

Company Motto:

We believe making class prices and services more available for parents and people who want to learn music will achieve more enrollment for Blue Bear School of Music.

Final thougts
Blue Bear School of Music is a respected organization in the Bay Area community. The site already reflected their character, we just focused on improving navigation so more people would complete the signup process. Was helpful to get feedback from the one of their program directors on their focus areas to help define scope and avoid scope creep, especially with the constrained timeline. Future Development We d recommend refining the Community section of the site as many of the pages are outdated and difficult to navigate. We did not include this in our initial scope as the sign-up process was our main focus.
